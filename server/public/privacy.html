Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to our website, managed by the BioComputing UP lab at the University of Padova. We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping your personal data secure. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, protection of your data, and your rights related to this data.

Information Collection and Use

Personal Data: We collect your first name, last name, ORCID (a unique digital identifier that distinguishes you from every other researcher, optional), username (optional), and password. Your names are used for personalization upon login and for identification in communications. The ORCID and username are unique identifiers for your account. We only require either ORCID or username; if ORCID is used, password entry is unnecessary as authentication is managed through their system.

Role-Based Data: We assign roles such as "visitor" and "curator" to users to manage access to different utilities of our web app. This information helps us to tailor user access and functionalities, ensuring users can only access features relevant to their assigned roles. For example, curators may have the ability to add new information to the database.

Data Storage and Security

Third-Party Storage: Your data is securely stored on MongoDB Atlas, a secure online database service. This provider is responsible only for the physical storage of data and does not access or interpret your data.

Security Measures: Our database is protected with strong access controls and encryption technologies to ensure your personal information is safely stored.

Data Access

Access Control: Data access is restricted to authorized personnel within the BioComputing UP lab who require this information to perform their duties.

User Rights

Your Data Rights: You may access, modify, or delete your data at any time by emailing biocomp@bio.unipd.it. To obtain a copy of your data, please contact this email as well.

Data Retention

Retention Period: We retain your data indefinitely to facilitate continuous access to our site without the need for re-registration.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Session Management: Cookies are utilized to maintain your session and authentication status, essential for seamless access to our services.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on consent obtained at registration and the necessity for the performance of the contract to which you are party by using our site. This processing also complies with legal obligations and protects your or others' vital interests as required by law.

Impact of Not Providing Data

Account Creation: Not providing required data (name, last name, and either ORCID or username and password) will prevent the creation of a user account, restricting access to our services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Notification of Changes: Any updates to our privacy policy will be communicated via a website banner upon your next login, ensuring you are always informed of how your data is handled.

Contact Information

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please reach out to us at biocomp@bio.unipd.it.

Effective Date

Implementation Date: This policy takes effect on January 1, 2024.
